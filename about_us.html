About BSIT & Program Offerings

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

A 4-year program at LCCM equipping students with technical and professional skills to design, implement, and manage information systems across industries.

Web Development

Hands-on experience in front-end and back-end development using industry-standard frameworks to build responsive web applications.

Cybersecurity & Networking

Courses cover network infrastructure, ethical hacking, data privacy law, and system administration — preparing students for roles in IT security.

Database Management

From relational databases to NoSQL systems, students learn to design, query, and maintain robust data architectures used in enterprise environments.

Elective tracks include AI & Machine Learning fundamentals, IoT, cloud computing, and data analytics — keeping graduates ahead of the curve.

Capstone & OJT

Culminating in a real-world capstone project and 500-hour on-the-job training, giving students direct industry exposure before they graduate.

Our Story
History of LITS

In response to the impact of the pandemic, students in the Information Technology program at La Consolacion College Manila needed a platform that balanced respect for hierarchies with open communication. As the pandemic ended, the League of Information Technology Students (LITS) emerged in August of 2023 as a beacon of new beginnings, fostering collaboration and knowledge exchange.

Formerly known as the "Junior Philippine Computer Society (JPCS)," the organization underwent a significant rebranding and regrouping phase to meet the evolving needs of students in the post-pandemic era. This marking a turning point allowed LITS to expand its reach and scope, focusing on enhancing the academic and professional growth of its members by providing opportunities for networking, skill development, and project collaboration.

The organization strives to maintain hierarchical respect while encouraging free expression and meaningful discourse. LITS supports its members in connecting, learning, and growing together, preparing them for future careers in the field of Information Technology. This approach ensures that the organization remains relevant and beneficial, fostering a vibrant and inclusive learning environment for all its members at LCCM.

Vision & Mission
Vision

Shaping the Future of Technology

To establish and preserve an inspiring extracurricular learning environment for aspiring Information Technology professionals, empowering students to flourish as digital pioneers and industry-ready experts. Our vision is to cultivate a community of learners who are not only responsive to the evolving needs of the IT industry but also dedicated to fostering a culture of academic excellence.

Mission

Cultivating Excellence Through Community

To empower Information Technology students through comprehensive education and practical skill development ensuring their readiness for excellence in their field. Our organization is committed to fostering a collaborative learning environment that nurtures innovation, creativity, and responsibility. By instilling a passion for learning and professional growth, our organization aims to cultivate future leaders who drive positive change within the Information Technology industry and beyond.
